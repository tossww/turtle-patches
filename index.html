How to play

  • Drag across squares to draw a patch around a sleepy turtle.
  • Each patch must have exactly as many squares as the turtle's number.
  • Every patch holds one turtle. Fill the whole board with no overlaps.
  • Tap a patch to remove it. Stuck? Use a 💡 hint.
